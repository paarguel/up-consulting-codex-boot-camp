# Money Review Checklist

Use this when an output includes costs, budgets, payments, invoices, reimbursements, or financial decisions.

- [ ] Are all amounts copied from a source file?
- [ ] Are totals recalculated correctly?
- [ ] Are assumptions clearly labeled?
- [ ] Are approvals required?
- [ ] Are vendor terms confirmed?
- [ ] Are payment methods or account details excluded?
- [ ] Is this safe to share with the intended audience?

Do not let Codex make final financial commitments.
