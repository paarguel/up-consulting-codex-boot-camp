# Final Send Review Checklist

Use this before sending, publishing, or sharing an output.

- [ ] Names are correct.
- [ ] Dates are correct.
- [ ] Times are correct.
- [ ] Locations and rooms are correct.
- [ ] Costs and quantities are correct.
- [ ] Contact information is correct.
- [ ] Private information is removed.
- [ ] The tone fits the audience.
- [ ] Unknowns are resolved or clearly marked.
- [ ] A human with authority has approved the message if approval is needed.
